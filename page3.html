I tried and tried to speak to your soul and heart but your pride and ego mistook me for being desperate. There were times l wanted to give up and let you go, but l always went back to that letter you wrote me, the words: “Please don’t give up on me.”

I remember that day you came over and gave me the letter. I remember the look in your eyes as l read it and the hug that came after. so l couldnt, she deserves to know someone wont just give up on her because things are hard.

So l stayed and tried again and again but for some reason l just couldnt reach my sweet girl.

I tried to hate you for all the things you said or did to me, but l couldn’t because deep down my sensitive little girl was still there somewhere, l just couldn’t reach her.

You are the same person l prayed to God for every night asking Him to keep us together and happy. The same person l promised my mum l would marry one day. The same person l promised your mom l’d protect and take care of for the rest of my life.

The same person l ran to the day after l tried to end it all. The same person who came to take care of me when l was sick. The same person who taught me how to be better and soo much more l could name for days.
