How I Loved You

I realised that I showed you I loved you too much, that I loved you to a point you felt you could do or say whatever you wanted and I would still love you the same.

At the beginning of our relationship I had a lot of pride and ego, but over time I realised I actually wanted to connect with you and learn parts of you no one else bothered to learn and I did.

I learned every part of you. I could tell how you felt just by looking into your eyes. I learned your likes, dislikes, dreams, goals, triggers and fears because making you smile became everything to me.

So I began to love you without holding back.

I fought and stayed this long not because I couldn’t do better or find someone else, but because I didn’t want better. I wanted you.
